Answers for every hamster parent

Everything you need to know about preparing for an overnight stay, from what to pack to how we keep your hamster happy.

What do I need to bring for my hamster's stay?

We provide fresh bedding, enrichment toys, and specialty treats. Please bring your hamster's current wheel (if they have a favorite), their primary food mix, and any medications or supplements.

How do you keep hamsters on their nocturnal schedule?

Lights are dimmed to red-spectrum bulbs in the evening, play sessions happen after dusk, and caretakers perform check-ins overnight to align with their natural rhythm.

Can you board multiple hamsters together?

Yes, as long as they already cohabitate safely. We create customized duplex suites with divider options, individual feeding zones, and shared play yards.

What happens if my hamster needs veterinary care?

Our caretakers are trained to spot early warning signs. If we notice anything unusual, we contact you immediately and coordinate with your preferred exotic vet, or our on-call partner clinic.

How often do I get updates?

You'll receive nightly photo updates, a morning recap, and optional livestream drop-ins for extended stays.

Do you accept last-minute reservations?

If we have availability, absolutely. We recommend booking at least a week in advance during holidays and summer travel seasons.

Still have questions?

Send us a note at hello@uberhamster.com or call 555-HAMSTER. We're happy to chat through the details.
